TOP 3 GOLDEN MOVIES OF ALL TIME

SHREK

I’ve sailed the seven seas, and you’re the sleekest schooner I’ve ever sighted. Is that a belayin’ pin in yer britches, or are ye …
Suddenly you’re like a pirate, you’re 65 years old and you’ve got an ear- ring.

SPONGEBOB SQUAREPANTS

Pardon me, but would ya mind if I fired me cannon through your porthole?
It is when pirates count their booty that they become mere thieves.

JOHN WICK

I’d love to drop anchor in your lagoon. Come show me how ye bury yer treasure, lad! And that was done without a single drop of rum… Pirate’s code:
First freedom and the captain.
Second the loot, third woman and the rum and at the end no mercy if they not immediately surrender! Without the messy paperwork.
